SUPERSEDED

Superseded is a publication about non-practicing architects in pursuit of other, non-architectural things.

Each issue features 10 individuals who have graduated architecture school with / without working experience. Superseded aims to explore the connections between their respective architecture backgrounds, their general feelings towards it and how it informs their decision to follow a path unrelated to architecture.

We are currently in the process of preparing our first issue.
